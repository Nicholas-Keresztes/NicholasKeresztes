About Me

Hi, I’m Nicholas! I’m from Surrey BC and I have always been interested in how technology and design work. Over time, I discovered my passion for web development, creating websites that look great and function smoothly. I enjoy turning ideas into interactive, easy to use pages and I’m always learning new skills to improve my work. My goal is to keep growing as a developer and build websites that are creative, modern, and meaningful.
